Roy Jossfolk is a Full Stack Developer with design and marketing skills. Currently living in Orlando, FL.

Over 10 years experience as the "go-to" growth hacker for start-ups and small businesses. I fell in love with the web back in 1997 and never looked back, learning everything from design and development to marketing and SEO. I have worked for and consulted with SaaS companies, early-stage startups and established businesses that sell products and services to Fortune 500 companies, institutions of higher learning and also professional athletes.

I bring unmatched passion and energy to everything I do. I wake up every day looking to make a difference in the products and projects I am working on. When a challenge presents itself, I come running. I believe in being efficient and effective. I am constantly staying up to date with the latest and emerging technologies via online training, meetups and social events.
